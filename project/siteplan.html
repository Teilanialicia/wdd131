Bali, Indonesia

Purpose: The site provides information about Bali, its landmarks or temples and some cultural and religious insights for those visiting for the first time, from Western-Asian individual who has visited Indonesia, was born in Indonesia and has Indonesian heritage.

Scenarios/Audience: The audience who would be lookign at this site would be people who are visiting Bali and want to get input on which sites to visit while there. These individuals would be interested in the religious/cultural background of the architecture and architectural landmarks, in order to experience the island to its fullest. Scenario one: Individuals already in Bali looking to plan some day-trips around the island. Scenario two: Individuals planning their trip beforehand and looking up how far each landmark is to the hotels they are booking.
